Our Vision

To innovate and lead in elevator security systems through the transformative power of electromagnetism, ensuring safer and more reliable vertical transportation solutions worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission at MagneGuard Solutions is to revolutionize elevator security systems by harnessing our expertise in electromagnetism. We are dedicated to developing and delivering innovative security solutions for electric elevators, utilizing cutting-edge technology and electromagnetic power to ensure unparalleled safety and reliability. Committed to excellence, we also aim to manufacture and supply high-quality electromagnet parts within Egypt, establishing ourselves as the leading supplier in the local market and contributing to the advancement of elevator safety standards in Egypt.
